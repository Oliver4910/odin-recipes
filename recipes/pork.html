Dr. Pepper Pulled Pork Sandwich

Description

Dr. Pepper would be proud of this hand crafted pulled pork recipe. Easy prep and cooked with an insant pot, its hard to find a better pork recipe out there.

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups caffeinated pepper-type soda
  • 2 cups barbeque sauce, divided
  • 1 pound pork tenderloin
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 2 cups prepared coleslaw

Steps:

  1. Combine soda and 1/2 cup barbeque sauce in a multi-functional pressure cooker (such as Instant PotÂ®). Add pork tenderloin. Close and lock the lid. Select high pressure according to manufacturer's instructions; set timer for 50 minutes. Allow 10 to 15 minutes for pressure to build.
  2. Release pressure using the natural-release method according to manufacturer's instructions, 10 to 40 minutes.
  3. Remove pork loin and drain liquid from the pot. Shred pork using 2 forks. Return to the pot and mix in remaining 1 1/2 cups barbeque sauce.
  4. Serve on hamburger buns and top with coleslaw.
